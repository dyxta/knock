01
What is Knock

The opportunity layer for SA students.

Knock is an AI-driven infrastructure that turns a student's verified academic transcript into a matchable identity — instantly connecting them to the right careers, programmes, and funding. WhatsApp-native delivery, POPIA-compliant, no extra app to install.

02
The vision

The singular, unbiased layer every SA student plugs into.

Our long game: one verified identity in → funding, programmes, and careers out. Every South African student plugged into the same opportunity layer, so potential gets recognised, funded, and hired without bias.

03
How this pilot works

100 verified UCT STEM grads. One WhatsApp drop. R2,500.

We collect transcripts from 100 UCT STEM final-years and verify them through our AI engine. You design the message; we deliver it directly to each student's phone. Within 7 days you get reach, click rate, and how your post performed.

04
Why now

SA youth unemployment is 60%+. Top STEM talent is invisible.

The graduate recruitment market is drowning in noise. We isolate the signal. Knock focuses on careers first, then we use that revenue to layer in funding and programmes. The pilot is how we prove the mechanic together. Low-risk for you, high-signal for us.
