what's the pilot?

knock is in early testing at a few universities. it's free to use. here's how it works:

👀
1. look
you get 3 free opportunities to look at right away. you don't need to sign up to see them.
♡
2. save
tap the heart on any opportunity you like. this saves it to a list so you can find it again later.
✓
3. sign up
fill in a short form (about 45 seconds). this adds you to your university's whatsapp group.
💬
4. get matched
twice a week, new opportunities are posted in your whatsapp group. that's the only time we message you.
🔒 your data: we only use it to put you in the right whatsapp group. we won't share anything that identifies you with companies unless we ask you first.
