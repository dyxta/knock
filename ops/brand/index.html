05 — Voice & Tone
How We Communicate
Knock speaks like a sharp colleague — direct, confident, and clear. We don't use corporate jargon or over-explain. We get to the point.
We are
Direct and confident
Concise — one idea per sentence
Action-oriented — verbs over nouns
Human — we talk to people, not audiences
Data-backed when making claims
We are not
Corporate or jargon-heavy
Overly casual or informal
Vague ("solutions", "synergies")
Exclamation-mark heavy
Passive voice

07 — Usage Rules
Dos & Don'ts
These rules exist so Knock always looks like Knock, regardless of who created the asset.

✓ Always

  • Use the period in "Knock." in all brand marks
  • Use Electric Purple (#5E17EB) as the sole accent colour
  • Use Cabinet Grotesk Black for headlines
  • Use SVG format for logos in digital contexts
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "K"
  • Use approved background colours only (black, white, purple)

✕ Never

  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Use the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
  • Introduce new brand colours
  • Use serif or decorative fonts
  • Use low-resolution (raster) logo files
  • Add drop shadows or effects to the wordmark
