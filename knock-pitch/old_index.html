Built for SA

Built for enterprise sovereignty.

Knock is not just a connector — it's a compliance routing layer built strictly for South Africa's regulatory frameworks.

Why it's the moat: The B-BBEE + POPIA scaffolding is the part that's hardest to copy and most expensive to build. Once corporates plug in, switching cost is real.

B-BBEE Integration

Automated routing of Skills Development and SED funds directly to student debt accounts. Companies secure tax points while unlocking premium talent.

Privacy & ethics (POPIA)

Democratised exposure removes implicit bias. PII is locked until explicit student consent is granted, guaranteeing audit-ready data.
