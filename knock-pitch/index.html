The Enterprise Unlock
B-BBEE · Code Series 300 · Statement 300
A legally mandated spend with no good solution — until now

Every large SA company must spend millions on skills development.
Most have no way to find the right students.

6%
of annual payroll — mandatory
A company with 500 staff at avg R400k salary = R12M/year legally required spend
2.5%
must go to student bursaries
Same company must put R5M/year specifically into students at universities — and most don't know who to give it to
25pts
= more contracts and revenue
Skills dev is 25 of 100 scorecard points. A better BBBEE level unlocks government tenders, corporate supplier programmes, and preferred client status — worth tens of millions annually
-1 lvl
= lose contracts overnight
Miss the skills sub-minimum and drop a full BBBEE level regardless of all other scores — Level 3 becomes Level 4. Major clients pull preferred supplier status immediately

The bottom line for any large SA company

You have a legal obligation to spend millions on student skills development every year. If you can't prove you spent it on the right students — in a traceable, auditable way — you lose BBBEE points, lose a level, and lose contracts. Knock is the platform that makes this spend trackable, targeted, and compliant.

What Knock delivers

✓ Find the right students by degree, GPA, and university — instantly
✓ Run and track skills workshops with a verified attendance record
✓ Full BBBEE audit trail — submit it at year-end, no spreadsheets
✓ Hire directly from the students you funded and trained
